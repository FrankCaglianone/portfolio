My Portfolio

TikTok-network-crawler

My bachelor research project explores the network dynamics of TikTok users engaged in climate activism. This software is versatile and can be adapted to analyze the network dynamics of users engaged in various topics. By modifying the initial set of users input into the algorithm, researchers can repurpose the tool to explore different areas of interest.

Homer Rush

A Mario Bros inspired video game but using Homer Simpon in Springfield. The goal is to make Homer arrive to Moe's bar, the end of the map, without falling. You walk on the road and can jump on donuts and Power Reactors. The gme contains dollars and skateboards, which respectively, increase and decrease the score counter.

CopenhagenBuzz

CopenhagenBuzz is an Android app that serves as a platform for sharing and exploring local events in the Copenhagen area, such as festivals and concerts. Users can view upcoming events, manage their favorite events, and interact with the app by adding, editing, or deleting events. It aims to connect locals and visitors with events around Copenhagen, enhancing community engagement and providing a platform for event discovery and management.
